Everything you need to know about setting up your instant automated WhatsApp plumbing triage dispatch channel.

Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account?

No. Our system manages the infrastructure channels. Alerts route cleanly via stable system webhooks, enabling you to receive standard formatted direct text updates to personal or business application numbers alike.

How accurate is the tool list recommendation?

The underlying processing layer runs on deep trade pattern data. While it can accurately flag the specific need for crimpers, high diameter valves, or water removal vacuums based on natural language or structural images, it serves as a rapid deployment reference point rather than an absolute legal diagnostic guarantee.

Can I link this form directly to my Google Ads?

Yes. The portal is fully optimized to run as a high performance landing page destination. You can drop your customized routing portal link straight into call extensions, local service campaigns, or organic map assets to transform high intent searches into immediate field dispatches.

Where are my customers uploaded images stored?

All incoming photographic media drops into isolated, heavily encrypted cloud vaults. They are processed within active runtimes and linked to your specific WhatsApp communication string, ensuring complete end to end processing privacy for residential site conditions.
