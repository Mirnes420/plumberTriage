The System

Three steps for the customer.
One instant alert for you.

From the moment a customer hits Submit, the entire pipeline — intake, AI analysis, WhatsApp dispatch — runs automatically.

1

Customer Submits the Form

A clean 3-step form collects everything needed without overwhelming the customer during a stressful emergency.

Name, phone number, full address Issue description in their own words Optional photo of the damage
2

AI Reads & Grades the Emergency

The description (and photo if provided) is passed to an AI engine. It classifies urgency, summarises the issue, and figures out which tools to bring.

Urgency: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW 1-line issue summary Recommended tools & parts list
3

Full Alert Hits Your WhatsApp

A structured message lands on your phone with the customer's name, address, direct navigation links, issue summary, gear list, and call-back number.

Google & Apple Maps links Customer photo forwarded if attached One-tap call-back number
