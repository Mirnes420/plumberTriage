FAQ

Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account?

No. Reports route directly to your standard personal or business WhatsApp. No API setup or third-party accounts needed on your end.

Can I use this as a Google Ads landing page?

Yes. The intake flow is built for high-intent emergency search traffic and converts well as a direct destination for plumbing emergency keywords.

What if the customer submits something vague?

The AI interprets the description and any photos attached to produce a structured report regardless. It will still flag severity and recommend likely tools based on what it can infer.

How long does setup take?

Most operators are live in a few minutes. Link your WhatsApp, share your intake URL with customers, and you are done. No technical setup required.
